Internship (2019)

Three of my friends from the same department in college and I interned at Digital Artisans Indonesia. The internship was a requirement to fulfill our academic credits. During this period, I took on the role of project leader and worked as a front-end developer. Together, we developed a proposal-making web app using Vue.js Mix and Laravel, with SQLyog as our database management tool.

We dedicated at least a month to this project. The website allows users to create proposals and automatically saves them to local storage to prevent data loss if the browser tab is accidentally closed. When reopening the app, it compares the data in the database with the local storage based on the timestamps, ensuring the latest version is used. We also included an export function to convert proposals into PDFs for printing if needed.
